       [中] 企业高级 (EA) 和其他收入——同比增长 13%，该细分市场的年度经常性收入 (ARR) 增长约 11%，主要由金融和技术垂直领域的现有客户推动。

  [ 32] [EN] INDUSTRY GLOSSARY
       [中] 行业术语

  [ 33] [EN] Atlas: MongoDB's fully managed, multi-cloud, database-as-a-service platform.
       [中] Atlas：MongoDB 的完全托管、多云、数据库即服务平台。

  [ 34] [EN] Enterprise Advanced (EA): MongoDB's commercial database offering for enterprise deployment, often operated on-premises or hybrid environments.
       [中] Enterprise Advanced (EA)：MongoDB 的商业数据库产品，用于企业部署，通常在本地或混合环境中运行。

  [ 35] [EN] ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue): The value of contracted recurring revenue over a one-year period from subscriptions or services.
       [中] ARR（年度经常性收入）：一年内来自订阅或服务的合同经常性收入的价值。

  [ 36] [EN] RPO (Remaining Performance Obligations): The value of future contracted revenue not yet recognized, typically in long-term contracts.
       [中] RPO（剩余履约义务）：尚未确认的未来合同收入的价值，通常在长期合同中。

  [ 37] [EN] FedRAMP High: Highest level of security certification required for U.S. federal government cloud usage, covering sensitive or mission-critical data.
       [中] FedRAMP 高：美国联邦政府云使用所需的最高级别安全认证，涵盖敏感或关键任务数据。

  [ 38] [EN] Vector Search: Technique for semantic information retrieval using embedding vectors, critical for AI-driven search and recommendation tasks.
       [中] 向量搜索：使用嵌入向量进行语义信息检索的技术，对于人工智能驱动的搜索和推荐任务至关重要。

  [ 39] [EN] Voyage: Refers to MongoDB's native feature for AI embeddings and related machine learning functions.
       [中] Voyage：指 MongoDB 的 AI 嵌入和相关机器学习功能的原生功能。

  [ 40] [EN] Agentic Workloads: Workloads enabled by agent-based AI systems that require dynamic data retrieval and real-time reasoning.
       [中] 代理工作负载：由基于代理的人工智能系统支持的工作负载，需要动态数据检索和实时推理。
