  [ 35] [EN] Commercial remaining performance obligation (RPO): The total value of contracted, yet-to-be-recognized revenue from commercial customers, typically reported with weighted average contract duration.
       [中] 商业剩余履约义务（RPO）：来自商业客户的已签约但尚未确认的收入总价值，通常与加权平均合同期限一同报告。  
原文：Commercial remaining performance obligation (RPO): The total value of contracted, yet-to-be-recognized revenue from commercial customers, typically reported with weighted average contract duration.

  [ 36] [EN] ARPU: Average revenue per user; key for analyzing revenue growth in subscription-based products.
       [中] ARPU：每用户平均收入；分析基于订阅产品的收入增长的关键指标。

ARPU: Average revenue per user; key for analyzing revenue growth in subscription-based products.

  [ 37] [EN] Finance lease: A lease arrangement in which Microsoft records the asset on its balance sheet, commonly used for data center sites and related infrastructure.
       [中] 融资租赁：一种租赁安排，Microsoft将资产记入其资产负债表，通常用于数据中心站点及相关基础设施。  
原文：Finance lease: A lease arrangement in which Microsoft records the asset on its balance sheet, commonly used for data center sites and related infrastructure.

  [ 38] [EN] Agentic AI / Copilot: Microsoft's AI-driven productivity systems and features facilitating workflow automation and task orchestration within organizational tools.

  [ 39] [EN] Tokens per dollar per watt: An internal Microsoft metric for measuring AI compute efficiency in relation to cost and power consumption.

Tokens per dollar per watt: An internal Microsoft metric for measuring AI compute efficiency in relation to cost and power consumption.
