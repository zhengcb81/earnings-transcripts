Industry glossary

CoCo (Cortex Code): Snowflake’s AI-driven code-generation and agent platform, enabling developers and partners to automate application and workflow creation within the Snowflake environment.

Snowflake Intelligence: The business user interface overlaying Snowflake’s platform, providing a natural language interface for data insight, actions, and AI-powered analytics.

Agentic control plane: Unified layer within Snowflake where business intent translates into governed actions across enterprise data, models, applications, and security policies.

Observe acquisition: Recent Snowflake acquisition contributing primarily personnel and limited product revenue, with a cited margin headwind.

Natoma: Targeted acquisition aimed at integrating application workflow automation and extending Snowflake’s AI capabilities directly into everyday business tools.
